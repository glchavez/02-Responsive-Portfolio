About Me

Hello, my name is Giovany Chavez and I am a current non-profit worker looking to get my foot in the door of the web development industry. Eager to take on this transition and make a positive career change, I have enrolled in the Denver University coding program. Despite the difficulty of this program, I am excited to take on this challange!

A little background on myself, aside from my interest in web development, I am a college graduate from Colorado State University with a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration. A Colorado Native, I moved to Washington D.C. after graduating for work and enjoyed my two years in the area. Missing family and my home state, I have recently moved back to Colorado.

Outside of work and learning, I really enjoy outdoor activities and sports. I am also a huge beer enthusiast and enjoy visiting different breweries when possible. A long-term goal of mine would be to open up a brewery.

I hope you enjoyed reading a little about me and looking at the work I've done so far!
